Welcome to our website!
Our project focuses on road safety and driver well-being by utilizing cutting-edge technology to detect driver drowsiness and alcohol limit while driving. Using Python and machine learning, our system provides early warning signals to drivers at risk of falling asleep at the wheel or driving under the influence. Equipped with an MQ-3 sensor and advanced facial recognition algorithms, the system detects the driver's alcohol limit and level of drowsiness and alerts them accordingly. Additionally, the system sends a text message to a registered person's number with vital information about the driver's condition and a link to our website. The website provides real-time information on the driver's location and a live stream of their actions to ensure immediate help if necessary. Our system is easy to use and promotes road safety, preventing accidents caused by drowsy or drunk driving.
